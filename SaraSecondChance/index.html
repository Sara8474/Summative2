WE REDUCE THE STRAY POPULATION

We save lives, offer a warm bed and a roof to sleep under. No dog should be left to fend for itself in the street, our mission is to do out part in reducing the population of strays by putting them in loving forever homes.

Less dogs on the streets means more happy and healthy dogs in loving homes. This is our passion.

WHAT IS MATCH MADE?

Match Made is a fun match maker adoption event hosted by us here at Second Chance.

You take a quiz which asks you about your personality, likes, dislikes and lving sitation, from here staff will personally find dogs which we believe suit you and your lifestyle. You have the option to participate in fun bonding activities and games with your matches!

If this sounds like something you're interested in, start by signing in to your Second Chance account, or if you dont have an account, you can sign up here!

At the end you may chose if you wanna spend extra time with whatever dog you please. This is a low stress, fun day out for the whole family, and the dogs love it!

WHAT IS THE QUIZ?

The test aims to figure out which of our dogs match you and your lifestyle the best. Your personality, routine, likes, dislikes, job situation, home and family are all taken into consideration when matching you with your potential matches!

WHY TAKE THE QUIZ

When adding a new dog to your family a lot needs to be considered! If you have other pets, children or elderly family members some dogs may not be suited for you, The size of your property and the time you have avalible to spend with you are also important. So let us help you find the right match.
